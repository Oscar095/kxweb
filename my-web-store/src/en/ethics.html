The Transparency and
Business Ethics Program

The Transparency and Business Ethics Program (PTEE) aims to establish internal policies, procedures, and codes of conduct that guide the actions of each and every employee of KOS Colombia SAS, in order to prevent acts of corruption and domestic or international bribery with counterparties — in accordance with Chapter XIII of the Basic Legal Circular and External Circular 100-000011 issued on August 9, 2021, by the Superintendencia de Sociedades (Colombian Business Registry Authority).

POLICIES

KOS Colombia SAS has established the following policies, which are aligned with the principles of Transparency and to which the company is fully committed:

  • 1. PTEE policies are mandatory for all stakeholders and counterparties and constitute rules of conduct that may not be violated under any circumstances.
  • 2. Any conflicts of interest between related parties shall be resolved in accordance with internal guidelines.
  • 3. KOS Colombia SAS will avoid entering into any type of relationship with suppliers and/or clients who appear on restrictive control lists related to Transnational Bribery or money laundering.
  • 4. KOS Colombia SAS will continuously promote an institutional culture against corruption, transnational bribery, and money laundering.
  • 5. The relationships of KOS Colombia SAS employees with their suppliers and/or clients shall be conducted in an atmosphere of cordiality, balance, and harmony in keeping with the spirit of this Policy.
  • 6. Any relationship with a domestic or foreign PEP ("Politically Exposed Person") must be validated by senior management in cooperation with the Compliance Officer and the Legal Representative, applying enhanced due diligence in all cases.
  • 7. Company employees at all levels must refuse to accept privileges, gifts, or invitations from suppliers and/or clients with whom they maintain a commercial relationship.
  • 8. KOS Colombia SAS, through its designated personnel and communication channels, will address inquiries, requests, or complaints from suppliers and/or clients in a professional and timely manner.
  • 9. KOS Colombia SAS will only conduct international and/or domestic transactions with third parties or potential contractors who are not linked to acts of domestic or transnational bribery, as reported on anti-corruption and/or money laundering control lists.
  • 10. The improper use of influence derived from position, role, or personal relationships with third parties — whether suppliers, clients, consultants, public officials, or any other party — is strictly prohibited.
