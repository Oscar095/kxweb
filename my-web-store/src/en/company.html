About Us

We are a company committed to environmental sustainability, specialized in the production and distribution of recyclable containers for hot and cold beverages. Our products are designed to deliver innovation, efficiency, and competitive pricing.

Mission

We are an organization dedicated to developing packaging and container solutions for the food industry, creating experiences that enhance the perceived value of our customers — in favor of environmental conservation — operating nationally and internationally for the benefit of all stakeholders, and adapting to the ever-evolving needs of the market.

Vision

By 2026 we aim to be an organization with strong brand recognition, leaders in food-industry packaging and containers — creating solutions that reduce environmental impact — recognized for our agility in meeting customer needs, with a market share exceeding 50% nationally and meaningful presence in international markets.
